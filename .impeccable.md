# Design Context — Pixtalase

## Users
Small-to-medium business owners and brand founders in Southeast Asia who are actively selling but feel their digital presence is scattered or underperforming. They browse on mobile and desktop, often during business hours. The job to be done: find a trusted web partner who will handle everything without requiring them to manage every pixel.

## Brand Personality
**Bold. Strategic. Human.** Pixtalase is a web agency that's confident without being cold, professional without being corporate. It speaks directly to the pain of being a growing brand that hasn't yet cracked the "look as big as you feel" problem.

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Light mode — warm off-white backgrounds create trust and readability, grounded in the brand palette.
- **Primary vibe**: Editorial-meets-professional. Bold numbered sequences, confident typography, generous whitespace punctuated by strong red accents.
- **Colors**: Drawn from Figma source (node 1:140):
  - Primary Red `#e62727` → `oklch(0.526 0.225 27)` — CTAs, numbered accents, bold moments
  - Accent Blue `#1a92bb` → `oklch(0.597 0.112 216)` — badges, secondary actions, trust indicators
  - Background Warm Off-White `#f3f2ec` → `oklch(0.965 0.009 85)` — page background
  - Border Gray `#dcdcdc` → `oklch(0.886 0.003 60)` — dividers, subtle borders
- **Typography**:
  - Display / headings: **Bricolage Grotesque** — variable-width grotesque with editorial confidence
  - Body: **Figtree** — clean humanist geometric, warm and readable
- **Anti-references**: Avoid generic SaaS templates with hero-metric stat cards, glowing gradients, or cyan-on-dark AI aesthetics.

## Design Principles
1. **Bold hierarchy first** — Every section should have one dominant visual element. Numbers, headings, or accent blocks that command attention before the eye reads body copy.
2. **Warm, not sterile** — All neutrals are warm-tinted toward the red hue. The background is never pure white.
3. **Red earns its place** — Primary red is used sparingly (CTAs, numbered steps, key highlights). Overuse kills its power.
4. **Trust through clarity** — Pricing, process steps, and outcomes are stated plainly. No vague "we do digital" language.
5. **Section contrast rhythm** — Alternate between warm off-white and near-black dark sections to create visual breathing room across the long scroll.
